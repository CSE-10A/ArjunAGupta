## Project Overview
A clean, professional personal website that highlights who I am, what I do, and what I want to become. The site will be easy to navigate and structured for colleges, internships, and future employers.

---

## 1) Front End Plan (Pages + Layout)

### A) Home / Landing Page
- **Hero section:** Name, short tagline, and a call-to-action button ("View About Me")
- **Quick links tiles:** About Me, Resume, Skills
- **Highlight strip:** Rocketry launch date, hackathon win, tennis captain
- **Background visual:** Space/tech themed image or clean gradient

### B) About Me Page
**Content layout:**
- **Intro paragraph** (professional tone, complete sentences)
- **Personal background timeline** (Lower Lab → Salk School of Science → Browning)
- **Interests & passions grid** (rocketry, AI, space, tech, reading, guitar, filmmaking, photography)
- **Athletics & leadership card** (tennis captain, flag football QB record, Tae Kwon Do black belt)

### C) Resume Page
**Resume-style sections:**
- **Education** (Browning; prior schools)
- **Relevant coursework & achievements** (Russian School of Math, hackathon win)
- **Extracurriculars** (rocketry, tennis, filmmaking/photography)
- **Experience & service** (Grassroots Grocery, orphanage book/stationery donation)

### D) Skills Page
**Three clear categories:**
- **Technical:** Python, Swift, Engineering
- **Academic:** Mathematics, problem solving
- **Personal/Soft Skills:** Leadership, teamwork, communication, creativity

### E) Navigation & Site Structure
- Top navigation bar with **Home | About | Resume | Skills**
- Consistent fonts and color palette across all pages
- Mobile-friendly spacing and readable typography

---

## 2) Back End Plan (Functionality)

**Site behavior and structure:**
- Multi-page static site (HTML/CSS/JS) OR single-page with anchored sections
- Navigation links jump to corresponding pages/sections
- Optional contact form (simple email link)
- Image assets stored in an `/assets` folder
- Fast loading, no broken links

**Future enhancements (optional):**
- Project gallery
- Downloadable resume PDF
- Contact form connected to email service
